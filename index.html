  • There is a strict ban on drinking in public places in Poland and police can impose fines of up to 100zl (about £20); of more concern is that it is technically illegal to be drunk in public and you could end up in the drunk tank. Tied to a bed after being stripped, so be advised not to push your luck with Polish police.
